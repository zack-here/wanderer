About Wanderers


This website is of great help to people who are interested in traveling to remote places. It helps tourists to book hotels in new places where tourism is not so developed and provides an overview about the surroundings of that spot. The website helps tourists book their hotels, sort out their overall tour and also provides for the lounge(food and drinks services) for the customers.

We provide all kind of tour packages from Kashmir in north to Kanyakumari in south. Separately hotels in various tourist spots can also be booked from our site. In these post covid days, the development of tourism in remote places has increased significantly. We, team Wanderers, would request all to visit all those remote places and spend some time with nature.



Team Wanderers:


It has been almost 2 years since the lockdown have been imposed due to covid-19. As those restrictions are being lifted everyone would like to go out of their daily 'work form home' and take a break from their monotonous schedule. Keeping all these in mind we, team Wanderers, have brought up this website.
